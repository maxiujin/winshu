What is Winshu? (Our first title)

Alke is the world's first Afro-Fantasy collectible card game. Alke is free-to-play and unlike other card games, Alke is not based entirely on luck and probability, instead players will have to duel while handling external variables that happens during their turns. In Alke you can summon any card with a summoning cost, making the game fast-paste and filled with combos.

These are Alkean cards. They are used for battle.

These are Uchawi cards, similar to spell cards in other games, use these cards to power up or destroy Alkeans.
